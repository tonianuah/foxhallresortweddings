ACTIVITIES



Sporting Clays



The Sporting ClaysCourse is set to be enjoyable for people of all ages and skill sets! You will love our state of the art 15 Station Beretta Trident Sporting Clays Course. No matter your group size or experience level, we have great sporting clays packages available to fit your needs. We are a full service outfitter, so we can provide you with everything needed to participate in sporting clays (targets, ammunition, gun rental, cart rental, and trapper). The sporting clays course is by far the most popular activity for wedding parties, especially groomsmen, to participate in. ​


BOOK NOW

ATV Rides



There's no better way to explore Foxhall than on an ATV! With over 16 miles of trails, there's plenty of things to see. Guided Tours of the property last 90 minutes including a safety instruction. Come see Foxhall like you've never seen it before! *You must be at least 16 years old with a valid Drivers License to operate an ATV.


BOOK NOW

Fishing



Looking to catch a trophy bass? With our first generation stock bass weighing in between the 10-15 pound range, your wedding group will want to join in on the fun and have their chance at catching the fish of a lifetime! With over 200 acres of unspoiled spring fed lakes, there are ample opportunities to wet a line from the shore or fish in a canoe, kayak or Jon boat for the next trophy bass.Whether you are looking for a Largemouth Bass in Lunker Lake, like the constant Coppernose Bluegill action in Eagle Lake, enjoy teasing the Rainbow Trout with a fly at Stone Lined Pond or have the heavy tackle rigged for the big Catfish in Legacy Lake, there is always something to catch at Foxhall.


BOOK NOW

Quail Hunting (October-March)



Want the thrill of the covey? Foxhall offers one of the south’s oldest traditions with well trained dogs and guides to help you have a successful hunt within only 30-minutes from Atlanta. The hunting grounds include over 2,000 plus acres of open fields, rolling hillside, pristine hardwood forest, and river bottom lands. They are managed and maintained by the Foxhall Outfitter with food plots, trail cameras and hunting stands placed throughout the property to take advantage of seasonal wildlife movement patterns.
