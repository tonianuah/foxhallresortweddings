ACTIVITIES



Sporting Clays



The Sporting ClaysCourse is set to be enjoyable for people of all ages and skill sets! You will love our state of the art 15 Station Beretta Trident Sporting Clays Course. No matter your group size or experience level, we have great sporting clays packages available to fit your needs. We are a full service outfitter, so we can provide you with everything needed to participate in sporting clays (targets, ammunition, gun rental, cart rental, and trapper). The sporting clays course is by far the most popular activity for wedding parties, especially groomsmen, to participate in.


BOOK NOW

ATV Rides



There's no better way to explore Foxhall than on an ATV! With over 16 miles of trails, there's plenty of things to see. Guided Tours of the property last 60 minutes including a safety instruction. Come see Foxhall like you've never seen it before! *You must be at least 16 years old with a valid Drivers License to operate an ATV. .


BOOK NOW

Fishing



Whether you are looking for a Largemouth Bass in Lunker Lake, like the constant Coppernose Bluegill action in Eagle Lake, enjoy teasing the Rainbow Trout with a fly at Stone Lined Pond or have the heavy tackle rigged for the big Catfish in Legacy Lake, there is always something to catch at Foxhall. A foxhall outfitter is here to help with your guided fishing trip or some basic pointers to help catch a fish of a lifetime.


BOOK NOW

Quail Hunting (October-March)



Want the thrill of the covey? Foxhall offers one of the souths oldest traditions with well trained dogs and guides to help you have a successful hunt within 30-minutes from Atlanta. There are currently three quail courses that can hold up to 12 hunters at one time.
